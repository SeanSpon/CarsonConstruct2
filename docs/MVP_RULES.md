## The 4 Quality Gates

Every clip must pass ALL FOUR gates. Fail any one = DROP.

- Must have at least 2 of 3 story elements:

  - **Resolution** (takeaway, conclusion)

### Gate 2: Visual Continuity

### Gate 3: Caption Clarity
