# Roadmap (Not Shipped)

This document lists future ideas and research only. Nothing here is shipped or promised in the MVP.

## Workflow and UX
- In-app video/audio preview for clips and dead space segments
- Real-time waveform and scrubbing
- Batch processing and queue management

## Distribution and publishing
- Social platform publishing (TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels)
- Platform-specific export presets

## Branding and captions
- Auto-captions with styling controls
- Intro/outro templates and watermark overlays

## Scaling and enterprise
- Multi-project batch workflows
- Team collaboration and approvals
- Audit-friendly exports for agency or enterprise workflows

## Detection and AI research
- Local transcription options and faster pipelines
- Speaker diarization and topic segmentation
- Sentiment and topic-based ranking
- Multi-modal analysis (audio + video signals)
